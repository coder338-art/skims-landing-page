FAQ

Questions answered

Do I need to give Lurke my Instagram password?

No. Lurke uses Instagram's official secure login (OAuth). We never see or store your password. Your credentials stay between you and Instagram.

How fast does Lurke detect unfollowers?

Lurke's engine checks for changes continuously and sends you a push notification the moment someone unfollows you or a new follower appears.

Will Instagram know I'm using Lurke?

Lurke only accesses your own account data through your logged-in session. It doesn't interact with other accounts, post on your behalf, or leave any trace.

Is Lurke free?

Yes, Lurke is free to download and use. A Lurke Pro subscription unlocks expanded features like full follower/following list visibility and advanced growth analytics.

Is Lurke available on Android?

Yes! Lurke is available on both the App Store for iOS and Google Play for Android. Download on Google Play.
